My Holiday

Written By: Miracle Shui Angela Situmorang - 2382007

Hello, my name is Miracle Shui Angela Situmorang. I want to tell you about My Holiday. During the past few months of vacation, I spent half of my time traveling with my family, visiting many places like the zoo near my house and various tourist attractions. One of the highlights was our trip to the zoo, where I encountered many cute and adorable animals. However, there was one animal that scared me, the white tiger. But when I felt afraid, my father hugged me and stayed by my side, encouraging me to take a closer look at the white tiger. With my father by my side, I eventually gathered my courage, and I even threw some meat to feed it. The tiger jumped so high to catch the meat that it looked quite adorable, and from that moment on, I grew to like the white tiger.

At the zoo, there were also animals I rarely see, like kangaroos, and they were incredibly cute as they hopped around. I was fascinated by their movements and spent time watching them play. It was a delightful experience that added to the joy of my vacation. The chance to see these unique animals up close was truly special, and it made me appreciate the diversity of wildlife even more.

Overall, I had an amazing time during my vacation, and I learned a lot about animals. The experience at the zoo was unforgettable, and it filled me with happiness. I truly enjoyed every moment of my holiday, and it was a time well spent with my family, creating memories that I will cherish forever.
